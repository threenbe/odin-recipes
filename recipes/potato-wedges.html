Ingredients

For the potatoes:

  • 2 pounds red potatoes -- about 6-8 red potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika (may sub in regular paprika)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Pinch of black pepper

For the dipping sauce:

  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1/3 cup bbq sauce
